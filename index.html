Frequently Asked Questions

Which language should I learn?

This is a question that only you can truly answer. Here at MEDUCAtE Centre we teach in over 10 foreign languages. It is really up to you, but if you aren't sure contact our team of specialists!

Am I the right age?

We have had students from 4 to 94 at MEDUCATE center. Age is no barrier to learning a new language, and it is extremely healthy and beneficial to learn a language at any point in your life.

What if I don’t have enough time?

Our courses are entirely flexible to your own timetable, and can be rearranged at no cost provided we are given 24hrs notice. If you prefer longer lessons less frequently, or short lessons, we can help! In addition, our teachers always make sure you have revision materials and work to do outside of classes to keep you in the zone.

What kind of course is right for me?

We offer both group and private courses at MEDUCATE centre. Our lessons allow you to receive bespoke, specialised tuition according to your own needs and ability, as well as your timetable, whereas group lessons allow you to meet other students and receive a collaborative learning experience.

What if I want to pass an exam?

Our teachers are familiar with all the major examinations and qualifications available in their language. So if you want to study specifically to pass a certain exam, whether it is the Chinese HSK or the IELTS, let us know and we can help!

I didn’t enjoy languages. Will I enjoy it now?

One of our main aims at MEDUCATE is to break away from the traditional classroom experience. Our teachers and staff aim to make your lessons as personalised, enjoyable and comfortable as possible.

What if I don’t ‘get’ languages?

In 5 years of teaching students, we have learned that there is no student the same. We have had language learners from all walks of life with all different experience levels. If you don’t think you ‘get’ languages, that is a myth we are on hand to dispel!

Is it expensive?

We provide different course lengths, formats and structures for any budget. Priced very competitively, they allow us to provide the highest quality language learning experience at our centre at the best value for you. For more info on our prices, check out our pricing page.

Can’t I just use apps or services only?

We love language learning apps and tools at MEDUCATE However, there is no substitute for face-to-face learning. While apps are fantastic at reinforcing vocabulary, the personalised tuition provided by a native speaker is invaluable, and better helps reinforce your learning. This is because it helps your brain form more absolute connections between words and experiences.

Why MEDUCATE Language Centre?

MEDUCATE Language Centre with over 10 years’ experience in providing high quality language tuition in a wide variety of formats. With native speaking teachers providing bespoke tuition at our training centre, there is no better place to start your language learning journey than MEDUCATE Language Centre. check out our testimonials for what our students have to say.
